About Simply Savvy

Located in Menomonee Falls, WI, Simply Savvy Charcuterie is your local destination for beautifully crafted grazing boards and custom charcuterie options. Whether it's for a wedding, a special event, or simply an indulgent evening, we focus on blending fresh, high-quality ingredients with a curated aesthetic that’s as pleasing to the eyes as it is to the tastebuds.

We believe that every gathering deserves a touch of elegance, which is why we offer a variety of options—each designed to elevate your experience. From our Char-Cutie Cups to the impressive grazing tables, Simply Savvy is here to make your occasion unforgettable.

Let us create something beautiful for you to savor.

Pickup & delivery available in Menomonee Falls and surrounding areas.
