About Simply Savvy

At Simply Savvy Charcuterie, every board is a carefully curated work of art — made to bring people together and make your moments memorable. Based in Menomonee Falls, WI, we specialize in crafting beautiful, custom charcuterie boards for gatherings big or small.

Whether it’s a cozy night in, a celebration with friends, or a corporate event, we believe in making every bite special. As a locally owned business, we focus on fresh ingredients, eye-catching presentation, and a touch of creative flair.

Simply put: simple, stunning, and totally satisfying.
